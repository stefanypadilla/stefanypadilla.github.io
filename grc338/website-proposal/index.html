Introduction

Blended Bliss

Welcome to Blended Bliss, a smoothie shop that sells blended fruit drinks, bowls, and small meals with and emphasis on providing customers with healthy, low fat, high-energy drinks!

Target audience

The target market for my smoothie business includes individuals and families who are seeking a cool, refreshing drink, anyone who is looking for a healthy drink, and anyone looking for a quick snack.

The goal of the business is to provide healthy, customizable smoothies, acai bowls, and food to customers who want quick-service meals with an emphasis on wellness.

About

In September 2012, the three of us, all long time friends (Stefany and Nayeli since childhood, Lexi since high school), had become enamored with the concept of smoothies. We were constantly looking for healthy, low fat, high-energy meals. We found that smoothies offered us exactly what we were looking for and tasted great. We also realized that we were not the only ones who felt that a smoothie was a perfect meal alternative.

Where? When? How?

We decided on the San Luis Obispo area because it was a place we knew and loved, and where active healthy life-styles seemed to flourish. Our name was Nayeli’s inspired choice to convey our emphasis on healthy, blended drinks.

Our Vision

As part of our development, we traveled around California visiting nearly every operating juice bar. Some shops had poor quality smoothies, some had bad service, and some had both.  Our vision evolved and soon crystallized. We would consistently provide the highest quality product in a clean, fun atmosphere, and in a quick manner.

For nearly 10 years now, Blended Bliss has curated famous flavors served by friendly staff. From smoothies, to smoothie bowls, to meals we got you covered! Fantastic flavor is always in season and nothing beats feeling your best. We look forward to sharing our fantastic flavors with you!

Our Local Commitment

Because we are part of a local community, we are committed to supporting local schools, athletic teams, and organizations through donations and sponsorships.
